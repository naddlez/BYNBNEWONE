My Design Process

1. PLANNING

This is the most important part of my process, I investigate what you need, I listen to your goals, dreams and ambitions. I think about it for a while and come back to you with something amazing: A Plan! How will my design relate to your target audience or theme? For a website, what features will it have? Can I make it something so magnificent and eyecatching and functional that people want to marry it?

2. DESIGN

I will sketch out some ideas or I will make you a beautiful site map so that you can see where I'm going with this whole crazy shebang. I will then make a mockup that blows your mind! (mind blowing conditions apply*)

3. DEVELOPMENT

Then I make magic happen before your eyes, on your computer screen. Wow, you might think, How did that mockup turn into something so aligned with my dreams and expectations? I have mad illustrator/photoshop/HTML/JQuery skills, yo!
I will keep you apprised every step of the way, That didn't produce my expected mindblowination, Why Nadia, Why? I will fix it.

4. DEPLOYMENT

When we are ready to print or go live - everything should be sparkly and up to your expectations. OK? Load time good? Check. Portrait of my dog looks like he's asking a question? Check. Logo animated to explode in people's faces? Maybe... Our design relationship is complete. Happy days are ahead!
