blue cottage



Located in the heart of the Sunshine Coast Hinterland, Blue Cottage is a holiday cottage defined by beautiful scenery, amazing beaches and proximity to the best the sunshine coast has to offer! Blue Cottage offers ecofriendly accommodation on a 10 hectare country property with wonderful views over the surrounding valley.


The cottage is fully self contained; cosy but spacious (accommodates up to 7) with :
Inside : 2 bedrooms upstairs, fully equipped kitchen with washing machine. Living area with TV;
Outside: a lovely gazebo and relaxing patio.

Wake to nature's sounds, breakfast in the gazebo, wander through organic orchard to shady creek, the pefect place to unwind after exploring markets or your day at the beach! You will feel completely rejuvenated & refreshed from your busy life as you relax in your own remote bushland retreat, yet be only 12 minutes from the coastal beaches, restaurants, shopping and nightlife!
